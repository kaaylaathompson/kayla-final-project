Philodendron

Philodendron spp.

Philodendrons are popular houseplants known for their lush foliage and ease of care.

Light- They thrive in bright, indirect light. They can tolerate lower light conditions but may not grow as vigorously. Avoid direct sunlight, as it can scorch the leaves. Place your philodendron near a window with filtered sunlight or in a well-lit room.

Water- Water when the top inch or two of the soil feels dry to the touch. Water thoroughly, allowing excess water to drain away from the bottom of the pot.

Pot- Choose a glazed ceramic pot, plastic pot, or hanging basket that is 1 to 2 inches larger in diameter than the root ball of your plant.
