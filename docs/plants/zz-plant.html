ZZ Plant

Zamioculcas zamiifolia

ZZ plants are fantastic low-maintenance houseplants, perfect for beginners or anyone who wants greenery without much fuss.

Light- Can tolerate low light conditions, but prefer bright, indirect light. Direct sunlight can scorch their leaves. They can also survive in artificial light.

Water- ZZ plants are drought-tolerant and prefer to dry out between waterings. Overwatering is a common mistake that can lead to root rot. Water them thoroughly, then let the soil dry out almost completely before watering again. Typically, watering once every 2-3 weeks is sufficient.

Pot- Keeping ZZ plants in their nursery pot is great, and planting in terra cotta or ceramic work, too. Whatever pot you use, make sure it has at least one drainage hole so the excess water can flow out.
