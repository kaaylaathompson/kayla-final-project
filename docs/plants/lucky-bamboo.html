Lucky Bamboo

Dracaena sanderiana

Caring for lucky bamboo is relatively easy, and it's believed to bring good luck and positive energy to its surroundings.

Light- Lucky bamboo prefers bright, indirect light but can also tolerate lower light conditions. Avoid placing it in direct sunlight, as this can scorch the leaves. If the plant is kept in low light, it may grow more slowly.

Water- Replenish your lucky bamboo with fresh water every seven to 10 days to keep it happy and healthy.

Pot- These plants aren't too picky about the type of container you choose. They can tolerate terracotta, plastic, glazed ceramic, and glass pots
